## Why 'Next Door' is Better than 'Across the Ocean'

Nearshoring moving manufacturing to Mexico isn't just a fancy business word. It’s a survival strategy.

- **Speed to Market:** Instead of waiting weeks for a container ship to cross the Pacific, your goods are now just a few hours away in Sonora.
- **Less Risk:** You don't have to worry about global politics or ocean storms. You just need a reliable truck and a clear path across the border.
- **The 'Made Near Me' Advantage:** Shippers in Phoenix and Tucson are finding that having their factory just a short drive away allows for better quality and faster changes.
